We focus on advising, consulting, and bringing venture projects to life. We have performed businesses turn arounds as well as many successful business expansions, mergers, and acquisitions in the areas of news media, radio, telecommunications, restaurants, entertainment, banking, and finance, among others in the Latin American Market.
